Help Center

How can we help you?

Need help with your order, payment, delivery, or account? Here are the most common questions and easy steps to get support quickly.

1. Track your order

If you already placed an order, go to the Track page and enter your order ID. You can see updates from when the restaurant receives the order to when the rider is almost at your door.

2. Payment questions

Choose your payment method at checkout: Bank Transfer, Card, Pay on Delivery, or Paystack. For bank transfer, follow the account details and confirm once your payment is complete.

3. Delivery and address help

Enter your full delivery address clearly at checkout. Include landmarks or building names to help the rider find you faster. If your rider is delayed, check the Track page for live updates.

4. Need support?

For live help, message us on WhatsApp at 09162124455 or email support@edgetech.ng. Our support team is ready to assist with orders, refunds, or any questions.

Quick tips for a smooth order

  • Choose a restaurant near your location for faster delivery.
  • Write a clear delivery address and add a nearby landmark.
  • Keep your phone available so the rider can contact you.
  • If payment fails, try another method or contact support.
