EdgeTech in the news

EdgeTech is transforming food delivery in Nigeria with faster service, secure payments, and partnerships that support local restaurants and riders. Below is the latest press story about our mission, growth, and impact.

A new standard for food delivery in Nigeria

Launched in 2023 by OKE ABDULWAHAB ADESHINA, EdgeTech began as a response to unreliable delivery and complicated payment experiences. Today, the platform is setting a new standard in Nigeria by combining restaurant partnerships, easy checkout, and live order tracking. EdgeTech provides customers with a fast, dependable service while helping restaurants expand their reach without extra complexity.

Supporting local restaurants and riders

EdgeTech is more than a delivery app — it is a platform built to support local businesses across Lagos, Ibadan, Port Harcourt, Oyo, and Osun. Restaurants benefit from reliable order processing and consistent customer flow, while riders get the tools they need to deliver more orders on time. This partnership-first approach creates value for every part of the delivery chain.

By working with trusted restaurant partners and investing in rider support, EdgeTech ensures every delivery meets high quality standards. This has led to stronger relationships, repeat customers, and measurable growth across the cities it serves.

Innovation in payments and tracking

Customers can choose from bank transfer, card payment, Paystack, or cash on delivery. EdgeTech’s flexible checkout options are designed to match real user needs while keeping every order secure. On the tracking side, customers can follow the order from restaurant acceptance to rider pickup and arrival, giving them peace of mind every step of the way.

What’s next for EdgeTech

EdgeTech plans to expand to more cities, add new restaurant partners, and improve delivery intelligence so customers get their food faster. The company’s long-term goal is to become Nigeria’s most trusted food delivery platform, offering reliable service without sacrificing quality.
