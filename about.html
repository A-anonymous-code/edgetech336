About EdgeTech

EdgeTech is Nigeria's premier food delivery platform, connecting hungry customers with delicious local cuisine from top restaurants across Lagos, Ibadan, Port Harcourt, Oyo & Osun.

How It Started

Founded in 2023 by visionary entrepreneur OKE ABDULWAHAB ADESHINA, EdgeTech began as a simple idea to solve the daily challenge of accessing quality food quickly and conveniently. Recognizing the gap in efficient food delivery services in Nigeria, Abdulwahab launched EdgeTech with a mission to revolutionize how Nigerians experience food delivery.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted and preferred food delivery platform, setting the standard for excellence in customer service, food quality, and delivery speed across the nation.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable food delivery services that connect customers with their favorite local restaurants, while supporting restaurant partners in growing their businesses and creating employment opportunities.

Our Goal

To expand our services to all major cities in Nigeria within the next 5 years, becoming the go-to platform for food delivery while maintaining our commitment to quality, speed, and customer satisfaction.
