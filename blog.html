Bringing the best of local food delivery to your door

At EdgeTech, we believe great food should be easy to order and even easier to enjoy. Our blog shares the story behind our service, our mission to support local restaurants, and the ideas that keep our delivery platform moving forward.

From a small idea to a local delivery leader

EdgeTech started with a simple goal: help busy Nigerians get hot, fresh meals from their favourite restaurants without the wait. Founder OKE ABDULWAHAB ADESHINA launched the platform after seeing how many people struggled with long delivery times and confusing payment options. Our company began by partnering with a handful of trusted restaurants, then quickly expanded across Lagos, Ibadan, Port Harcourt, Oyo, and Osun.

Today, we continue to grow while keeping the same focus on reliability, speed, and customer happiness. Every delivery is a chance to show why EdgeTech is the best choice for food lovers who need fast, local service.

Supporting restaurants and riders every step of the way

We work closely with restaurant partners to help them reach more customers and manage orders smoothly. Our platform makes it easier for restaurants to receive accurate order details, prepare meals efficiently, and get paid quickly. At the same time, EdgeTech provides delivery riders with the tools they need to navigate busy streets and deliver orders on time.

This means better experiences for customers, stronger partnerships for restaurants, and more reliable work for riders who deliver quality service every day.

Why EdgeTech is different

EdgeTech is built for customers who want speed without sacrificing quality. We keep our platform simple, our communication clear, and our support team ready to help if anything goes wrong. Whether you’re ordering bank transfer, card, Cash on Delivery, or Paystack payments, we want the checkout process to feel smooth and secure.

By focusing on local restaurants and local cities, EdgeTech can tailor delivery routes, support faster restaurant onboarding, and maintain excellent service even as we grow.

Looking ahead

Our goal is to become the most trusted food delivery service in Nigeria, one order at a time. We are expanding carefully, improving our technology, and adding more cities so customers can enjoy the same fast service wherever they are. The future includes smarter order tracking, wider restaurant selection, and even better support for every customer.

Thanks for reading—if you’re hungry, we’re here to deliver.
