Become a Rider

Join EdgeTech as a delivery rider

We are building a strong team of riders across Nigeria. If you are ready to deliver great food, earn steady income, and support local communities, we want to hear from you.

Rider opportunities

As a rider with EdgeTech, you’ll deliver orders from restaurants to customers across Lagos, Ibadan, Port Harcourt, Oyo, and Osun. We welcome reliable, customer-friendly riders who know our cities and want to earn while helping people get their meals fast.

How to reach out

Riders should contact us directly using our support channels. Send a message with your name, city, and availability to:

  • WhatsApp: 09162124455
  • Email: riders@edgetech.ng

You can also visit our office if you prefer to speak in person. We will respond quickly with the next steps, rider onboarding details, and how to start accepting deliveries.

What we look for

Ideal riders are punctual, respectful, and ready to communicate with customers. You should have a working phone, familiarity with local routes, and the ability to handle food safely.

Why ride with EdgeTech?

EdgeTech offers flexible delivery work with consistent order flow and direct support. You’ll be part of a team focused on helping customers enjoy food faster and supporting restaurants with reliable, professional delivery.
